## Full Description
**ARENA is an intense, minimalist survival arcade game designed for pure mechanical skill (Reflex Boost) and visual rhythm.**

Enter a high-contrast cyber-world where every move matters. Control your glowing craft with precision touch inputs and navigate a relentless storm of hostile red nodes. There are no power-ups to save you—only your reflexes, your timing, and your mastery of the ARENA.

### KEY FEATURES:
- **Reflex Boost System:** Stack multipliers by dodging near enemies. High risk, high reward.
- **Pure Skill-Based Gameplay:** No RNG, no pay-to-win. Just you against the machine.
- **Cyber-HUD Visuals:** Deep black backgrounds perfectly paired with high-contrast neon elements for a sleek, premium aesthetic.
- **Responsive Controls:** Engineered with a native-driver input system for latency-free movement.
- **Dynamic Difficulty:** The ARENA adapts to your survival time, continuously scaling the intensity to match your skill level.
- **Designed for Tablets:** Optimized for all aspect ratios, from small phones to large iPads.
