# Play Store Submission Guide for ARENA

Follow these steps in the [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console) to ensure a smooth review process.

## 1. Privacy Policy Setup
Google requires a **URL** for your privacy policy. 
- **Option A (GitHub Gist):** Go to [gist.github.com](https://gist.github.com), paste the content of `PRIVACY_POLICY.txt`, and click "Create public gist". Use the "Raw" link or the Gist URL.
- **Option B (GitHub Pages):** If you have a GitHub repo, you can host the file there.
- **Action:** In the Play Console, go to **App content** > **Privacy policy** and paste your URL.

## 2. Data Safety Form
When filling out the **Data Safety** section:
- **Data Collection:** Select **"No"** for "Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?".
- **Security:** Select **"Yes"** for "Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?".
- **Deletion:** Select **"Yes"** for "Do you provide a way for users to request that their data be deleted?". (Even if you don't collect data, this is a safe default for "Delete Account" requirements).

## 3. Content Rating
- Select **"Game"** as the category.
- Answer **"No"** to all questions regarding violence, sexuality, and restricted substances (unless your game contains them).
- For ARENA, it should typically receive a **PEGI 3** or **ESRB Everyone** rating.

## 4. Target Audience
- Select **13 and older** (to avoid the complexities of the "Families Policy" unless you specifically want to target younger children).
- If you select and age under 13, you must comply with strict COPPA/GDPR-K rules.

## 5. Functional App
- Ensure you have uploaded the **App Bundle (.aab)** generated by `eas build --platform android --profile production`.
- Provide **Logins/Test Accounts** if your app has a login screen (not needed for ARENA as it's a direct-play game).

## 6. App Metadata
- **Short Description:** A high-speed survival arcade game.
- **Full Description:** Use the professional description from `PLAY_STORE_LISTING.txt`.
- **Screenshots:** Upload at least two high-quality screenshots for Phone and Tablet.

## 7. Compliance Declarations
Go to **App content** near the bottom of the sidebar and complete these specific forms:
- **Financial Features:** Select "My app does not provide any financial features".
- **Health Apps:** Select "My app is not a health app".

## 8. Regional Availability
If you get an error saying "No countries or regions have been selected":
1. Go to **Production** (or your testing track).
2. Click the **Countries/regions** tab.
3. Click **Add countries/regions** and select **"All"** (or specific ones you want).

## 9. Deobfuscation (R8/ProGuard)
The warning about "No deobfuscation file" can be ignored for your initial release. However, if you want to fix it:
1. Go to your **EAS Build** dashboard.
2. Download the **"Mapping file"** or **"Symbols"** from the finished build artifacts.
3. Upload it in the Play Console under the App Bundle options.
